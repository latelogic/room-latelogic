TL;DR — Why Hyatt Category 4 Still Slaps in 2026

  • Hyatt preserved a 15k standard cap while Marriott and Hilton went dynamic — meaning you still have a ceiling to anchor value.
  • Category 1-4 free night certificates from the World of Hyatt Credit Card, Bilt Rent Day, and Sweet Elite Milestones can routinely unlock $500–$900 cash stays.
  • Daily housekeeping, resort fees waived, and 2pm late checkouts (Globalist) still apply when you book through points or RoomPoints discovery.

Understanding Hyatt Category 4 Rules (2026 Refresh)

If you’re new to the Category 4 conversation, here’s what changed — and what didn’t — this year:

  • Standard room awards remain 12k/15k/18k for off-peak/standard/peak. Hyatt announced no devaluation for 2026, so budgeting 15k per night still works if your dates are flexible.
  • Certificates ignore peak pricing. Drop a Cat 1-4 free night certificate on peak nights and still pay zero points, provided the property hasn’t moved to Category 5.
  • Fifth-night-free? Not here. That’s a Marriott + Hilton perk. Instead, Hyatt gives you Guest of Honor bookings (elite perks share), Milestone rewards, and confirmed suite upgrades. Pairing a certificate night with those benefits is how you beat the system.
  • Cash + Points (6k points + 50% cash) can also sneak under the Category 4 ceiling when standard awards are gone but inventory still exists.

Top Category 4 Sweet Spots by Region

Below you’ll find our 2026 all-stars. Each property includes real world pricing snapshots pulled directly from RoomPoints members.

1. North America City Breaks

Perfect for maximizing long weekends, elite qualifying nights, or stacking with companion airfare deals.

  • Thompson Austin (Texas, USA) — routinely $520+ with taxes, but bookable for 15k points. Use it for Formula 1, South by Southwest, or ACL Fest when cash prices surge.
  • Hyatt Regency Vancouver (BC, Canada) — $480 CAD summer rates plus 15% tax. RoomPoints alert data shows 17 nights of 15k availability in June/July 2026.
  • Andaz Ottawa ByWard Market (Ontario, Canada) — A boutique stay with rooftop views of Parliament. Cash nights are $430 CAD; certificates cover even Canada Day.

Why it works: Hyatt still treats premium downtown locations as Category 4 if the room count is high. Business-heavy hotels like Austin’s Thompson collapse on weekends, making award space plentiful.

2. Mexico & Caribbean Resorts

Hyatt quietly added boutique all-inclusives, but the legacy Cat 4s remain unstoppable for families.

  • Hyatt Zilara/Ziva Cancun (Adults / Families) — When they drop to Category 4 in off-peak months, you’re getting $680 rooms plus all-inclusive dining for a single certificate.
  • Andaz Mayakoba Resort (Playa del Carmen) — Technically toggles between Category 4 and 5, but 20+ nights each September still load at 15k points.
  • Hyatt Regency Grand Reserve Puerto Rico — Massive suites, waived resort fees, and a golf course view that sells for $550 cash.

Pro Tip: Transfer Chase points instantly once RoomPoints pings availability. These resorts vanish within hours because free breakfast + no resort fees is unheard of in Cancun.

3. Europe Culture Hubs

Hyatt’s European footprint is still smaller than Marriott’s, but the quality per point is higher.

  • Hyatt Centric Milan Centrale — Next to Milano Centrale with rooftop aperitivo lounge. Cash rates hit €480 during Fashion Week; certificates ignore the surge.
  • Hyatt Regency Paris Etoile — Technically Category 5, but watch for extended maintenance windows when it temporarily prices as Category 4. RoomPoints flagged three multi-day windows in January 2026.
  • Story Hotel Riddargatan, part of JdV (Stockholm) — Design-forward boutique that rarely shows points availability publicly. RoomPoints surfaces hidden inventory at 15k in shoulder season.

4. Asia-Pacific Luxury for Pennies

Asia remains the easiest region to crush CPM (cents per point) valuations because cash rates stay high but Hyatt refuses to reclassify.

  • Grand Hyatt Kuala Lumpur — $360 cash weekdays, $220 weekends, but club lounge access + skyline infinity pool make it a $600 experience. Certificates clear almost every month.
  • Hyatt Regency Kyoto — Base rooms include 600 sq ft tatami suites that sell for ¥68,000. Book during cherry blossom season for unmatched value.
  • Alila Manggis (Bali) — Beachfront eco-luxury, typically 8k–12k points, but still eligible for Category 4 cert top-off nights. Stacks perfectly with Bilt Rent Day bonuses.

5. Business Travel Power Plays

Sometimes you just need a dependable property that keeps expenses low but still impresses clients.

  • Hyatt Place London City East — Trendy co-working vibe, breakfast included, and $410 cash rates midweek. Use certificates to pad elite nights while cutting T&E budgets.
  • Hyatt Regency Seattle — Convention center adjacent, 1,260 rooms, and 15k awards even during AWS re:Invent overflow weeks.
  • Hyatt Centric South Beach Miami — Rooftop pool + gym partnership with Exhale. Weekend rates push $580; certificates book a “SUNSET KING” with partial ocean view.

New for 2026: Hidden Suites and Guest of Honor Loopholes

Hyatt quietly rolled out two updates, both of which tilt in your favor:

  1. Standard Suite Awards Reclassified. Select properties now list junior suites as “Standard.” If a Category 4 hotel does this, your 15k redemption drops into a 650 sq ft suite without needing a Globalist upgrade.
  2. Guest of Honor via Bilt. Rent Day status matches now allow non-Globalists to gift Guest of Honor bookings on certificate stays. That means guaranteed free breakfast + late checkout for your parents without you being on the trip.

FAQ: Rapid-Fire Answers for 2026

Did any fan-favorite properties move up to Category 5 this year?

Yes: Park Hyatt Siem Reap and Hyatt Centric Waikiki both jumped. If you’ve been hoarding certificates, pivot to the remaining list above ASAP.

Can I stack a Category 4 certificate with a Milestone Suite Upgrade?

Absolutely. Certificates simply replace the points component of the stay; suite upgrades attach to your reservation number regardless of payment type.

What about all-inclusive properties?

Hyatt Zilara/Ziva resorts are capacity-controlled and often bucketed at Category 4 outside school holidays. The catch is that Hyatt sometimes restricts certificate usage to minimum three-night stays. Watch the fine print.

When should I transfer Chase UR to Hyatt?

Only when you have a specific booking in mind. Hyatt points are stable, but any devaluation risk should be matched with immediate usage.

Is there still value in buying Hyatt points?

When Hyatt runs a 25% bonus sale, you’re effectively paying ~1.92¢ per point. That’s still a win for properties delivering $500 nights at 15k points (3.3¢). Anything below 2.1¢ remains a green light in our tracker.
