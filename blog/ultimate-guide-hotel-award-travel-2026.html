The Big Four Programs (2026)

While dozens of hotel loyalty programs exist, four matter to most travelers due to their footprint and partner credit cards. Understanding their personalities is step one.

1. Marriott Bonvoy

  • Footprint: 8,000+ properties worldwide.
  • Why we care: Variety—from Courtyard to St. Regis—and Fifth Night Free on awards.
  • Watch out: Fully dynamic pricing. Keep an eye on peak dates.

2. World of Hyatt

  • Footprint: Smaller (~1,300 properties) but premium heavy.
  • Value: Points worth 1.8–2.5¢, real award chart, waived resort fees.
  • Downside: Limited footprint in smaller cities.

3. Hilton Honors

  • Footprint: Massive, similar to Marriott.
  • Strength: Easy elite status, Fifth Night Free.
  • Weakness: Lower point value (~0.5¢).

4. IHG One Rewards

  • Footprint: Broad presence in mid-tier & luxury (InterContinental, Six Senses).
  • Highlights: Fourth Night Free with card, refreshed elite benefits.
  • Gotcha: Resort fees often still charged on awards.

Earning Points Without Sleeping in Hotels

The secret of the hobby is that everyday spending earns more points than actual hotel stays. Here's how savvy travelers build balances fast:

Credit Card Welcome Offers

A single premium card bonus can net 100K+ points—enough for 3–5 nights at luxury properties. Always plan your minimum spend to avoid debt.

Category Bonuses

Use cards that earn 3–5x on dining, groceries, and travel. Rotate cards strategically for shopping seasons.

Shopping & Dining Portals

Never shop online directly. Instead, click through Amex/Chase portals to stack bonus points with your card earnings.

Buying Points (Sparingly)

Only buy during 80–100% bonus promos and when you have a redemption in sight. The math must beat the cash rate.

Transferable Currencies = Flexibility

The golden rule: bank points are safer than hotel points. Keep balances in Chase, Amex, Citi, or Capital One until you're ready to book.

Chase Ultimate Rewards

  • 1:1 transfers to Hyatt (best value), Marriott, IHG.
  • Pair Sapphire Preferred/Reserve with Freedom cards.

Amex Membership Rewards

  • 1:1 to Hilton (often 30-50% bonuses) & Marriott.
  • Strong lineup of premium cards with statement credits.

Citi Strata Premier

  • Unique access to Choice Privileges—great for Europe.

Capital One Miles

  • 1:1 to Wyndham & Choice. Venture X offers lounge access.

Redemption Math & Modern Sweet Spots

Not all redemptions are equal. Use this rule of thumb: (Cash Rate - Taxes) / Points = Value Per Point. Chase transfers to Hyatt should target >1.8¢ per point, Hilton stays should yield >0.5¢.

Example: Park Hyatt Kyoto

Cash: $900/night · Points: 35,000 · Value: 2.57¢/pt. Add Globalist benefits (free breakfast) and the value climbs higher.

Example: Waldorf Astoria Maldives

Cash: $2,500/night · Points: 120,000 · Value: 2.08¢/pt + no resort fees. Fifth Night Free takes it below 100k/night.

Advanced 2026 Tactics

Fifth Night Free & Fourth Night Free

Marriott and Hilton let you pay for 4 nights and get the 5th free. IHG Premier cardholders get every fourth award night free.

Free Night Certificates (FNCs)

Most issuers now allow you to "top off" certificates with up to 15K points. This unlocks aspirational properties.

Waived Resort Fees

Hyatt and Hilton waive resort fees on full award stays—worth $50–$100 per night in Hawaii or Vegas.
