Travel shouldn't just be for the wealthy.

RoomPoints started with a simple frustration: credit card points are confusing. Banks sell you on "free travel," but when you try to use your points, you find blackout dates, absurd fees, and terrible value.

We built RoomPoints to level the playing field. By aggregating real-time award inventory from over 150 airlines, we give normal travelers the same tools that expert travel agents use.

"Our mission is to help 1 million families take a dream vacation they otherwise couldn't afford."
